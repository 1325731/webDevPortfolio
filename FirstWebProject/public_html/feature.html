What can GameMaker do?

Here I'll talk about 3 important features that GameMaker uses in order to run a game, a sprite, an object, and a room. There are other important things GameMaker can use but aren't fully required which are:

  • Sounds
  • Backgrounds
  • Paths
  • Scripts
  • Shaders
  • Fonts
  • and Time Lines

What are Objects?

Objects are used to execute drag and drop or code functions in your game.


~ GameMaker and Objects ~

What are Rooms?

Rooms are the location where your games may take place within.


~ GameMaker and Rooms ~
